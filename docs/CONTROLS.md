# Controls

## Steer the ship

`W` `A` `S` `D` move the Mk-77 through space. Use them to dodge hazards drifting toward you: picket buoys in Sector I, asteroid shards in Sector II, gate spines in Sector III.

## Aim the reticle

Move the mouse, or use the arrow keys. The ship's movement and the reticle aim are independent — you can steer with one hand and aim with the other.

## Fire

Left mouse button, `Space`, or `F`.

## Precision

Hold `Left Shift` or the right mouse button to slow aim and sharpen shots.

## Hazards

Hazards are not enemies. They cannot be shot. Dodge them with `WASD`. Contact drains shield first, then hull. A brief invulnerability window prevents back-to-back damage on the same glancing blow.

## Other

`P` pauses during play.

`R` restarts after game over.

`F12` saves `capture.bmp`.

`Esc` quits.

The OS cursor is hidden while the game is running — the in-game reticle is your cursor.

## Demo Mode

Run `./run.sh --demo` for a fast-starting curated demo. It immediately enters play, steers past hazards, tracks targets automatically, fires with a quick cadence, and shows the visual hook without waiting on title-screen input.
