# Prompt 10 — Audio, pacing, and vertical slice polish

Make the game feel presentable.

Add:
- simple but effective audio hooks using SDL audio or a lightweight SDL audio path
- shot sound
- enemy hit/destroy sound
- warning/danger tone
- title/game over cues if feasible

Then tune:
- spawn timing
- scoring loop
- wave pacing
- moments of tension and relief
- difficulty ramp in the first few minutes

Goal:
- this feels like a real arcade slice, not a rendering demo

Verify:
- starts fast enough
- escalates cleanly
- remains readable
- gives a reason to replay
